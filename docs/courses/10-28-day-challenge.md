# Module 10 — 28-Day AI Challenge

**Code path:** `src/data/content/modules/10-challenge-28-day-ai/`

## Purpose

While Modules 1–9 build capability, Module 10 builds habit. It provides a structured one-lesson-per-day challenge that spans foundations, productivity, career, business, personal life, and integration — designed to establish a sustained daily practice of applying AI skills across professional and personal domains.

## Curriculum Position

Module 10 is the capstone module in the catalog. It is designated as `courseType: "challenge"` with `durationDays: 28`, signaling different engagement expectations than standard modules. The personalization algorithm includes this module in recommended paths for learners who report generous daily time availability (20+ minutes) through the time-for-challenge override rule.

## Pedagogical Approach

The 28 days are organized into thematic blocks that maintain cognitive separation between domains. Foundations and prompting are placed in the first week so learners develop operational skills early. Weekly review checkpoints are incorporated to consolidate learning and prevent later days from feeling disconnected from earlier material. The module concludes with systems integration and a forward-looking 90-day planning exercise.
