# Module 9 — Workflow and Automation

**Code path:** `src/data/content/modules/09-workflow-automation/`

## Purpose

This module integrates the individual tool skills from Modules 5–8 into coherent multi-step workflows. Rather than using AI tools in isolation, learners apply tool-chain thinking to complete end-to-end professional tasks such as researching a topic, drafting documents, generating graphics, and assembling deliverables.

## Curriculum Position

Module 9 follows the four tool-specific mastery modules and precedes the 28-Day Challenge (Module 10). It represents the synthesis phase of the curriculum: combining previously learned capabilities into integrated professional workflows. The personalization algorithm maps this module to the `workflows` identifier and routes learners with time pressure or workload stress toward it through the time-based override rule.

## Pedagogical Approach

Lessons progress from workflow fundamentals and model selection criteria through daily workflow patterns, compound research processes, audio and long-document handling, third-party integrations, strategic planning, and best practices. Each lesson emphasizes iteration speed and practical applicability for time-constrained professionals.

## Structure

- 8 chapters, 30 lessons, approximately 450 minutes total estimated duration.
- Chapter progression: Essentials, Model Selection, Daily Workflows, Compound Research, Audio and Documents, Integrations, Strategy, Best Practices.
