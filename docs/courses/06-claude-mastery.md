# Module 6 — Claude Mastery

**Code path:** `src/data/content/modules/06-tools-claude/`

## Purpose

This module provides instruction for Anthropic's Claude, with particular emphasis on the platform's strengths in long-context processing, project-based knowledge management, and careful prose generation. It is designed for professionals whose work involves research, extended documents, and structured information packages.

## Curriculum Position

Module 6 is positioned as a parallel to Module 5 (ChatGPT). After completing the ChatGPT module, learners can compare tool strengths and identify contexts where Claude is more appropriate — particularly for research synthesis, long-form drafting, and sustained context across multiple documents.

## Pedagogical Approach

The module mirrors the structure of other mastery modules: essentials and professional applications first, followed by Claude's differentiating features (projects, knowledge bases, artifacts), advanced capabilities, and team integration patterns.

## Structure

- 6 chapters, 30 lessons, approximately 430 minutes total estimated duration.
- Chapter progression: Essentials, Professional Applications, Advanced Features, Projects and Knowledge, Advanced Deep Dive, Integrations and Team Patterns.
