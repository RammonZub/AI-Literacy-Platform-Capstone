# Module 2 — AI Productivity

**Code path:** `src/data/content/modules/02-skills-productivity/`

## Purpose

This module bridges conceptual AI understanding (Module 1) with operational competence. It teaches learners how to apply AI tools to daily professional tasks — writing, research, note-taking, meetings, and document preparation — with an emphasis on repeatable workflows rather than isolated techniques.

## Curriculum Position

Module 2 is designed to follow Module 1 and precede formal prompt engineering (Module 3). It translates foundational awareness into time-saving habits grounded in real work artifacts. The personalization algorithm frequently routes beginners and independent professionals to this module through the beginner and independent profession override rules.

## Pedagogical Approach

The module follows a three-part progression: improving prompt quality for common tasks, establishing research and communication loops, and integrating AI into weekly work routines. Each lesson emphasizes patterns that can be repeated across different tools and contexts.

## Structure

- 3 chapters, 9 lessons, approximately 140 minutes total estimated duration.
- Learning outcomes: structured prompting habits, document and research workflows, personal AI integration plan.
