# Module 4 — Content Creation

**Code path:** `src/data/content/modules/04-skills-creation/`

## Purpose

This module teaches learners to use AI as a collaborative partner for professional content production — writing, reports, presentations, and multi-format communication. The emphasis is on maintaining voice consistency, editorial judgment, and quality assurance rather than delegating output entirely to AI.

## Curriculum Position

Module 4 applies the prompting skills from Module 3 to creative and commercial writing contexts. It assumes basic AI familiarity and prompt construction ability. The personalization algorithm routes independent professionals and content-oriented roles toward this module through the independent profession override rule.

## Pedagogical Approach

Lessons cover the full content lifecycle: planning, drafting, repurposing across formats, and structured review. AI is positioned as an accelerator for ideation and first drafts, with human judgment serving as the quality gate. Lessons also introduce lightweight image-prompt literacy as preparation for Midjourney (Module 8).

## Structure

- 1 chapter, 10 lessons, approximately 120 minutes total estimated duration.
- Learning outcomes: AI-assisted drafting workflows, brand-aware content collaboration, planning and review processes.
