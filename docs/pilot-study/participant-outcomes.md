## Aggregate Outcomes

- **Lesson completion:** 40 / 43 = **93.0%** (H1 threshold ≥ 70%)
- **Return rate:** 8 / 12 = **66.7%** (H2 threshold ≥ 50%)
- **Mean SUS:** **75.83** (H3 threshold > 68)
- **Unassisted task completion:** 47 / 48 = **97.9%** (H4 threshold ≥ 90%)
- **Recommendation relevance:** **8.33 / 10** (H5 threshold ≥ 7.0)
- **First-session activation:** **100%** overall, 83.3% unassisted (H6 threshold ≥ 80%)
- **NPS proxy:** (6 − 2) / 12 × 100 = **+33**
